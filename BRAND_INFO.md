## SOPs

### Tools used
- **Gohighlevel:** CRM for managing customer data and communication
- **Google Calendar:** For scheduling and managing jobs. Each job is stored as a seperate event. The event time represents the arrival window (eg, 8-9am, 9-10am, 2-4pm). The event title is the customer name, and the description is all the details about the job. 
- **Google Sheets:** For tracking finances and other business metrics. 
- **Yelp:** primary source of leads, where a lot of the communication starts. 
- **Google Local Service Ads:** second main source of leads, where communication is handled through the Google LSA interface.
